Instructions

  1. This return is to be used by non-Saudi resident individuals who exercise business in the Kingdom and keep books and records.
  2. A taxable activity includes activity in commerce, industry, agriculture, services, banking, insurance, investments of all types, transportation operations and rental of movable, immovable, tangible or intangible assets, and any vocational, professional or other similar activity for profit.
  3. Opening bank accounts or trading of stocks in the Saudi Stock Market alone is not considered an activity. However, if such income is derived along other income, it should be reported with the other income.
  4. This return should be filed and payable tax be paid within 120 days of the end of the taxpayer's financial year.
  5. Failure to file the return or pay as prescribed above will cause a non-filing penalty as per Article (76) of the Income Tax Law, and a delay penalty of 1 % of underpayment for every 30 days of delay as per Article (77), paragraph (a) of the Income Tax Law.
  6. Documents proving payments should be attached with this return.
  7. The taxpayer is fully responsible for the correctness of the return data.
  8. A chartered accountant should certify the correctness of this return if gross income exceeds one million Saudi Riyal.
  9. To be complete, this return should have all fields completed, required schedules and attachments attached.
  10. This return should be filled out in clear Arabic. No erasure, deletion or alteration to data of the return is allowed.
  11. Additional clarifications should be attached with the return.
